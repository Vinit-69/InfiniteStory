Getting Started with InfiniteStory

Welcome to InfiniteStory! This tutorial will guide you through the basics to help you start your adventure. Lets dive in!

Understanding the Basics

InfiniteStory is an interactive text-based game powered by advanced AI. You'll make decisions, solve puzzles, and navigate through a rich, evolving story.

Starting the Game

Create an Account: Click on the "Log in" button to log in.

Start New Game: Once logged in, click "Start New Game" to begin your adventure.

Navigating the Game

Text Interface: The game primarily uses text to convey the story and interactions. You will read descriptions and make choices through text input.

Reading Descriptions: Pay attention to the descriptions provided. They will guide you through your current situation and offer clues about what to do next.

Making Choices: AI will present you with some choices based on the scenario, you can choose out of those, or you can enter your own thing in prompt and that will count as your choice.
