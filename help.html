Getting Started

Welcome to InfiniteStory! Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Visit the Website/App: Go to the InfiniteStory official website or open the app.
  2. Sign Up: Click on “Sign Up” and enter your email address, username, and password.
  3. Verify Your Account: Check your email for a verification link and follow the instructions.
  4. Log In: Use your new credentials to log in to the game.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions:

  • Cannot Log In: Ensure your username and password are correct. Check your internet connection. If you’ve forgotten your password, use the “Forgot Password” link to reset it.
  • Game Crashes: Make sure your device meets the game’s requirements and that you have the latest updates installed. Try restarting the game or your device.
  • Unable to Connect to Server: Check your internet connection. The servers might be down for maintenance. Try again later.

Tips for New Players

  • Explore the Tutorial: The tutorial provides essential information to help you get started. Pay attention to the tips and instructions.
  • Join a Community: Engage with other players in forums or social media groups for advice, strategies, and companionship.
  • Regularly Check Updates: Keep an eye out for game updates and patch notes for new features and improvements.

Common Questions

How do I change my username?
Username changes can be requested through the account settings page. Please note that there may be restrictions or a fee associated with changing your username.
What should I do if I encounter a bug?
Report the bug through the in-game support system or contact our support team via email. Provide as much detail as possible to help us resolve the issue.
How can I purchase in-game currency?
In-game currency can be purchased through the store section of the game. Follow the prompts to choose your preferred payment method and complete your purchase.
